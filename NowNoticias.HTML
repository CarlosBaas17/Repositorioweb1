Italy's referendum: A nightmare scenario in the heart of Europe

Such a scenario would require a line of political dominoes to fall in just the right way. But investors are increasingly concerned. The yield on 10-year Italian government bonds has spiked in recent weeks above 2%. The yield on German debt is just 0.3%.
